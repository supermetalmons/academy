# Puzzles

Here is a collection of puzzles designed to test your Super Metal Mons skills!

Note: The goal for each of these is not a direct win, but to find the turn that guarantees victory on your next turn, no matter what Black does.

Note: Some of these have not been rigorously playtested and may have unintended solutions or errors.
