# Super Metal Mons FAQ

#### What is Super Metal Mons?
Super Metal Mons (SMM) is an abstract strategy game of perfect information (a bit like chess) with an assortment of collectible monsters, originally featured in a pair of NFT collections on Ethereum created by [Supermetal Bosch](https://x.com/supermetalx).

#### Who made the Super Metal Mons game?
Bosch designed the board game together with his brother, [Turtle](https://x.com/turtletimezone). [Ivan Grachyov](https://x.com/ivangrachyov) is the developer of mons.link.

#### Where can I play?
[mons.link](https://mons.link/)

#### Do I need a crypto wallet to play?
No. The game is fully playable without connecting a wallet. If you do connect (ETH and SOL both work), you can earn ELO rating by winning automatches and see your rank on the global leaderboard.

#### Is the game still in development?
The foundations of SMM are complete, but the current mon types (Drainer, Angel, Spirit, Mystic, and Demon) and items (Bomb and Potion) comprise only "Base Set 1". New pieces with unique abilities will be rolled out over time, giving players the option to customize their team loadout to their playstyle. There are many additional features planned for mons.link — stay tuned.

#### Is there a physical version of the game?
Yes. Production is currently paused, but once resumed they will be available [here](https://www.supermetalmons.com/collections/all/).

#### What is Little Swag World?
[Little Swag World](https://littleswag.world/) is another NFT collection created by Bosch. They're kind of like the trainers of metal mons.
